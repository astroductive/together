FAQ

Common questions.

Does Together require an internet connection?

The AI models run entirely in your browser using TFLite (English ASL) and PyTorch (Arabic ArSL). Once loaded, sign recognition works fully offline. Live meeting sessions require connectivity for WebRTC signaling only.

Is my camera feed sent to your servers?

Never. All video processing happens on your device using MediaPipe Holistic running in WebAssembly. Only anonymized text predictions can be shared in live sessions — and only when you choose to.

Which sign languages are supported?

Currently American Sign Language (ASL) with 250 sign classes and Egyptian Arabic Sign Language (ArSL) with 20 classes. We are actively training models for Gulf ArSL and British Sign Language (BSL).

How accurate is the recognition?

Under controlled lighting with good camera framing, ASL accuracy exceeds 92% top-1 across the 250-class vocabulary. ArSL reaches 89% on the 20-class set. Accuracy improves with a neutral background and sufficient light.

Can I use Together in a video call?

Yes. The Live Meeting module uses WebRTC via Socket.IO. Share a session link and captions appear for all participants live. Supports up to 8 participants per session.

How do I book a demo for my organization?

Visit the Book a Demo page or email us at hello@together.app. We offer 30-minute live walkthroughs for accessibility teams, educational institutions, and enterprise clients.
