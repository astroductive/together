Enterprise

Educational

Free or subsidized deployment for schools, universities, and special education programs. Teacher dashboards, student progress tracking, curriculum integration.

Research

NGO & Advocacy

Free licenses for registered Deaf-led organizations, advocacy groups, and accessibility NGOs. Community deployment support included.

How to apply

Fill in the contact form below or email partnerships@together.app. Include a brief description of your organization, the use case, estimated user scale, and which partnership type fits best. We respond within 3 business days.

For research partnerships, attach a one-page research summary. For enterprise, mention your industry and any compliance requirements (HIPAA, FERPA, GDPR, etc.).
