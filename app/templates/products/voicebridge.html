How it works

VoiceBridge chains HandScript's recognition output directly into the Web Speech Synthesis API. As HandScript emits a confident prediction (above the configurable confidence threshold), VoiceBridge queues the word or phrase for speech. The synthesis uses the browser's built-in TTS voices — no external API call is made, preserving offline capability.

A 200ms phoneme-based debounce prevents partial signs from triggering early utterances. Sentence boundaries are detected heuristically by sign-pause gaps longer than 600ms, at which point the buffered text is flushed as a complete utterance with natural prosody.

Use cases

Pharmacy & healthcare

Ask for a medication, describe symptoms, or follow up on results — hands-free, voice-forward, without paper.

Travel & public services

Navigate airports, train stations, and government counters with confidence. VoiceBridge speaks your signs to staff.

Customer service

Retail, banking, and food service counters equipped with a tablet running VoiceBridge become accessible to Deaf customers instantly.

Social & casual

Dinner tables, parties, casual hallway chats — VoiceBridge makes spontaneous conversation natural again.

Voice customization

VoiceBridge exposes the full Web Speech API voice roster — on most platforms this includes a dozen or more natural-sounding voices across genders and accents. The selected voice and speech rate are persisted to localStorage. A dedicated Voice panel in the dashboard settings lets users audition each option.
